Boss tier perks and benefits

Health Pack

We take care of our team with comprehensive Medical (HMO or PPO), Dental, and Vision plans at no cost for employees.

Parental Leave

Paid leave for new parents to spend some quality time with the next generation of gamers!

Take a Load Off

Stay refreshed with 14 paid holidays, use-what-you-need sick days, and four weeks of PTO.

Commute easy

Fully subsidized commuter and parking benefit (up to $265/month). Get over here!

The Loadout

Awesome daily catered lunches and snacks. A desk fund to make your space yours. Whatever you need for your computer setup, including a headphone allowance.

It’s Fitness Time

Workout on us! A fitness allowance for your gym membership or favorite workout class. Yoga in office every Thursday.

Insurance Suite

Basic Life & AD&D, Disability, Supplemental Life, and Accident insurance.

Discord HQ

Our office is located in Potrero Hill, a little neighborhood in SF near awesome coffee, food, and Whole Foods. Also sushi.

Relocation

Excellent customized relocation packages for new hires.
